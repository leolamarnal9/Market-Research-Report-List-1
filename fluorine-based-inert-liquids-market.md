<p><p>The fluorine-based inert liquids market primarily comprises per- and polyfluoroalkyl substances (PFAS) and perfluoropolyether (PFPE) compounds. PFAS are known for their chemical stability and resistance to heat, making them suitable for applications in firefighting foams, coatings, and lubricants. PFPEs, on the other hand, are highly stable and used in aerospace, electronics, and high-performance lubricants due to their exceptional thermal and chemical resistance. Both categories cater to various industries requiring reliable and enduring fluid solutions.</p></p>
