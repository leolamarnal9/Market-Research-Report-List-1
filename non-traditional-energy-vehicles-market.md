<p><p>The non-traditional energy vehicle market encompasses various types of eco-friendly transportation options. Blade Electric Vehicles (BEVs) rely solely on electric power and battery storage. Extended Range Electric Vehicles (EREVs) combine electric propulsion with an auxiliary internal combustion engine for extended range. Hybrid Electric Vehicles (HEVs) integrate both electric and conventional fuel systems for improved efficiency. Fuel Cell Electric Vehicles (FCEVs) generate electricity from hydrogen and emit only water. Hydrogen Powered Vehicles utilize hydrogen as fuel, promoting clean energy and reduced emissions in transportation.</p></p>
