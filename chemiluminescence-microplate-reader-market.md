<p><strong>The Chemiluminescence Microplate Reader Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Glow Type</li><li>Flash Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Chemiluminescence Microplate Reader market is primarily divided into two types: Glow Type and Flash Type. Glow Type readers detect continuous light emission from luminescent reactions over time, offering high sensitivity and ease of use for applications like enzyme-linked immunoassays. Flash Type readers, on the other hand, measure brief light bursts produced by rapid chemical reactions, providing rapid readout and enhanced dynamic range for applications requiring high throughput or real-time analysis. Each type serves distinct research needs in laboratories.</p></p>
