<p><p>The medical tubing market comprises various materials tailored for specific applications. PVC (polyvinyl chloride) is known for its versatility and cost-effectiveness, making it popular in disposable items. Polyolefin offers superior flexibility and chemical resistance, ideal for a range of medical applications. TPE (thermoplastic elastomer) and TPU (thermoplastic polyurethane) provide elasticity and durability, suitable for advanced medical devices. Silicone is biocompatible and flexible, used in critical applications like catheters. Other materials include rubber and nylon, which serve niche requirements in the medical sector.</p></p>
