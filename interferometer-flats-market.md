<p><p>Interferometer flats are precision optical components used in interferometry to provide a reference surface for measuring wavefront distortion. Market types include λ/10, λ/20, and others, indicating the wavefront accuracy relative to the wavelength of light. A λ/10 flat has a maximum deviation of one-tenth of a wavelength, making it suitable for high-precision applications. A λ/20 flat offers even greater accuracy, while other types may serve specialized needs in areas like metrology or optical testing, balancing cost and performance.</p></p>
