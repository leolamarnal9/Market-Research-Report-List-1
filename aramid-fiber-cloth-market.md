<p><ul><li>Woven Cloth</li><li>Knitted Cloth</li><li>Non-woven</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aramid fiber cloth market comprises three main types: woven, knitted, and non-woven fabrics. Woven cloth features interlaced fibers, providing high strength and durability, ideal for applications like protective gear. Knitted cloth, made from interlooped yarns, offers flexibility and comfort, suitable for clothing and technical textiles. Non-woven fabrics are produced by bonding fibers through heat or chemical processes, resulting in lightweight, porous materials primarily used in insulation, filtration, and composite applications. Each type serves distinct industrial and consumer needs.</p></p>
