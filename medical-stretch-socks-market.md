<p><p>The medical stretch socks market primarily includes gradient compression socks and anti-embolism socks. Gradient socks provide varying levels of compression, typically higher at the ankle and gradually decreasing upward, enhancing blood circulation and reducing swelling. Ideal for active individuals and those with circulation issues, these socks support vein health. Conversely, anti-embolism socks are designed for patients who are immobile or recovering from surgery, preventing blood clots by maintaining adequate blood flow in the legs while ensuring comfort and safety.</p></p>
