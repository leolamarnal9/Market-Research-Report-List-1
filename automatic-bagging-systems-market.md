<p><p>Automatic bagging systems are categorized into semi-automatic and fully automatic markets based on their level of automation and operational efficiency. Semi-automatic systems require manual intervention for certain tasks, such as loading bags or adjusting settings, making them suitable for medium-scale operations. Fully automatic systems, on the other hand, operate independently with minimal human interaction, featuring advanced technology for high-speed bagging, precise filling, and automated handling. These distinctions cater to varying production needs and investment capabilities in the packaging industry.</p></p>
