<p><p>The air drying purification machine market includes two primary types: Purge Air Systems and Breathable Air Systems. In contrast, Breathable Air Systems ensure the air quality meets safety standards for respiratory use, filtering out harmful particles and providing clean air for industrial and medical applications. Both systems play crucial roles in maintaining air quality across various industries.</p></p>
