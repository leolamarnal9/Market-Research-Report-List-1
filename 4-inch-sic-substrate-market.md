<p><p>The 4-inch SiC substrate market comprises two main types: conductive and semi-insulated. Conductive substrates enable efficient electronic applications due to their high electrical conductivity, often used in power devices and high-frequency applications. In contrast, semi-insulated substrates exhibit controlled conductivity, minimizing unwanted current leakage and enhancing the performance of microwave and RF devices. Both types play a crucial role in advancing semiconductor technology, with applications spanning from electric vehicles to renewable energy systems, driven by the demand for efficient power management solutions.</p></p>
