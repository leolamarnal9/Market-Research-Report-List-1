<p><p>The aeroengine composites market comprises three main types: polymer matrix composites (PMCs), ceramic matrix composites (CMCs), and metal matrix composites (MMCs). PMCs, favored for their lightweight and versatile properties, are extensively used in fan blades and casings. CMCs, known for their excellent temperature resistance and low weight, are ideal for hot sections of engines. MMCs combine metals and reinforcements to enhance strength and thermal stability, making them suitable for various aeroengine components, contributing to efficiency and performance improvements.</p></p>
