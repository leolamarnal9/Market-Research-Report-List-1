<p><p>Healthcare EDI (Electronic Data Interchange) market can be categorized into two main types: stand-alone systems and integrated systems. Stand-alone systems operate independently, focusing solely on EDI functions like claims processing and billing, often favored by smaller practices due to their simplicity. Conversely, integrated systems combine EDI capabilities with broader healthcare management solutions, allowing for seamless data sharing across various functions such as patient management and electronic health records (EHR). This integration enhances workflow efficiency and improves overall care coordination.</p></p>
