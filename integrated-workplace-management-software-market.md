<p><p>Integrated Workplace Management Software (IWMS) is categorized into two primary market types: on-premises and cloud-based solutions. On-premises IWMS involves installing software directly on an organization's servers, offering greater control and customization, though requiring significant IT resources for maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based IWMS is hosted on external servers, enabling easier access, scalability, and reduced upfront costs. This model allows organizations to leverage ongoing software updates and collaboration features, promoting flexibility in managing facilities and workplaces efficiently.</p></p>
