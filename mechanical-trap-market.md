<p><ul><li>Ball Float Type</li><li>Semi Ball Float Type</li><li>Lever Float Type</li><li>Inverted Bucket Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mechanical trap market includes several types of traps used for steam and condensate management. Ball float types utilize a floating ball to control discharge based on water level. Semi ball float types offer a similar mechanism but with a semi-fixed float for better responsiveness. Lever float types utilize a lever system for more precise control over condensate flow. Inverted bucket types trap air and allow condensate to escape when a bucket rises. Each type optimizes efficiency and reliability in various applications.</p></p>
