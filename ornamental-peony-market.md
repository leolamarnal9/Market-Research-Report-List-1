<p><p>The ornamental peony market primarily features two types: Paeonia suffruticosa and Paeonia lactiflora. Paeonia suffruticosa, or tree peonies, are valued for their woody stems and large, lush blooms, typically offering vibrant colors and fragrances, making them popular in landscaping and gardens. In contrast, Paeonia lactiflora, known as herbaceous peonies, are renowned for their ease of cultivation and wide variety of colors. Both types are sought after for their aesthetic appeal and are widely used in floral arrangements and garden design.</p></p>
