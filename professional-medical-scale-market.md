<p><p>The professional medical scale market encompasses two primary types: medical mechanical scales and medical electronic scales. Medical mechanical scales utilize traditional analog mechanisms, relying on weights and levers for accurate measurements, making them durable and easy to maintain. In contrast, medical electronic scales employ advanced technology, featuring digital displays and electronic sensors for precise weight readings, often with additional features like data storage and connectivity. Both types cater to healthcare settings, ensuring accurate weight measurement for patient assessments and treatment plans.</p></p>
