<p><p>A contactor relay is an electromechanical device used to control electrical power circuits. It consists of a coil, which generates a magnetic field when energized, and one or more sets of contacts that either open or close to control the flow of electricity to connected devices. These relays are widely used in various applications, including industrial automation, motor control, and power distribution due to their ability to handle high voltage and current levels.</p><p>The Contactor Relay Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.2% during the forecast period, driven by increasing industrial automation, rising demand for energy-efficient systems, and the growth of renewable energy sources.
