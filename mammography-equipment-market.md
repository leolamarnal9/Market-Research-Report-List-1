<p><ul><li>Analog Mammography Equipment</li><li>Digital Mammography Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mammography equipment market is divided into two main types: analog and digital mammography equipment. Analog mammography uses traditional film to capture images, requiring chemical processing, which may limit image accessibility and quality. In contrast, digital mammography utilizes digital detectors to convert X-ray signals into digital images, allowing for immediate viewing and enhanced analysis through advanced software. Digital systems generally provide higher image clarity, improved diagnostic capabilities, and greater storage options, making them increasingly preferred in clinical settings.</p></p>
