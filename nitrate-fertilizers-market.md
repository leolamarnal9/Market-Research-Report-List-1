<p><p>The nitrate fertilizers market encompasses various types, including Calcium Ammonium Nitrate (CAN), Ammonium Nitrate (AN), and Calcium Nitrate (CN). CAN combines calcium and ammonium nitrate, promoting healthy plant growth and improving soil structure. AN is a highly soluble nitrogen source, ideal for boosting crop yields. CN supplies both calcium and nitrogen, enhancing nutrient absorption and reducing soil acidity. Each type caters to specific agricultural needs, driving demand in the market for efficient nutrient management and sustainable farming practices.</p></p>
