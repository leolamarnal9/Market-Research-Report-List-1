<p><p>The acne prescription medication market can be divided into two main types: topical and oral. Topical medications, such as gels, creams, and lotions, are applied directly to the skin and work by targeting localized acne. They often contain active ingredients like retinoids, antibiotics, or benzoyl peroxide. Oral medications, including antibiotics, hormonal treatments, and isotretinoin, are systemic and address acne from within the body, making them suitable for more severe cases. Both types aim to reduce inflammation, acne lesions, and future breakouts.</p></p>
