<p><p>Aviation Turbine Fuel (ATF) is primarily categorized into kerosene-type and naphtha-type fuels. Kerosene-type fuels, such as Jet A and Jet A-1, are the dominant choice for commercial aviation, known for their high energy density and low freezing point, making them suitable for high-altitude flights. Naphtha-type fuels, though less common, are utilized in specific applications like certain military aircraft and some business jets, offering characteristics that cater to particular operational needs. Both fuel types play vital roles in aviation efficiency and safety.</p></p>
