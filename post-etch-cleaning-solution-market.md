<p><p>The post etch cleaning solution market primarily includes two types: aqueous and semi-aqueous solutions. Aqueous solutions use water as the primary solvent, effectively removing residues without leaving harmful contaminants, making them suitable for various substrates. In contrast, semi-aqueous solutions combine water with organic solvents, offering enhanced cleaning capabilities for challenging residues while minimizing drying times. Both types play crucial roles in semiconductor manufacturing, enhancing the quality and performance of electronic devices by ensuring clean surfaces before subsequent processing.</p></p>
