<p><p>Solder Paste Inspection (SPI) systems are vital in electronics manufacturing to ensure the quality of solder paste application. In-line SPI systems integrate directly into the production line, allowing real-time monitoring and immediate feedback to optimize processes. Conversely, off-line SPI systems operate separately from the main production line, enabling batch inspections of PCB assemblies. Each type has distinct advantages: in-line systems enhance efficiency and reduce defects, while off-line systems provide comprehensive analysis and flexibility for detailed assessments.</p></p>
